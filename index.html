About Me

I am a senior hardware engineer with total of 8 years of professional experience, including roles at Bosch, a leading Tier-1 automotive supplier, and at Monolithic Power Systems (MPS) as an Application Engineer automotive design experience at Bosch. I have done my Master's in Microsystems Engineering from the University of Freiburg, Germany, specializing in analog mixed-signal IC design.

Projects

  • Two-Stage Amplifier Design

    Designed, simulated, verified, and laid out a CMOS two-stage amplifier in XFAB 350nm technology using Cadence Virtuoso. The workflow included MOS transistor sizing based on design specifications, schematic entry, and extensive simulations (DC, AC, transient, Monte Carlo, and process corner analysis). Completed full layout implementation followed by parasitic extraction, LVS checks, and post-layout verification to ensure performance compliance.

  • Low-Frequency Signal Analyzer & Generator

    Developed a USB-powered audio-band (20 Hz – 20 kHz) signal analyzer and signal generator. Designed the schematic, performed LTspice simulations for circuit validation, and executed PCB layout for a 4-layer board with proper grounding and EMI considerations. Oversaw fabrication and testing of the PCB to achieve reliable performance across the target frequency range.
